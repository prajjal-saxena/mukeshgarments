ABOUT US

MUKESH GARMENTS was incepted in year 1981 with the aim of providing a complete range of technical and industrial textile products. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company & a member of Indian Technical Textile Association (ITTA). It is one of the leading manufacturers, suppliers & exporters of geotextiles in the country. The company gives out its best quality textile products to a number of clients in india and international market. We have a fully automatic plant with all the modern machines, testing equipment & infrastructure facilities. Virendera Textiles is engaged in manufacturing, supplying and exporting a wide product range which has rendered the company a respected name in its sphere.
